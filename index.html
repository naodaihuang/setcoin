SET Coin

SET Coin is a decentralized protocol for redistributing wealth, supporting basic financial functions like payments, gifts, and microfinance through a Smart UTXO architecture. It is designed for inclusion, justice, and simplicity, aiming to bring billions of unbanked individuals into the global economy.
